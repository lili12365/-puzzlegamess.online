Contact

Contact PuzzleGames.space

Use this page for support questions, gameplay page corrections, accessibility notes, rights concerns, or requests related to how a title is presented on PuzzleGames.space.

Primary contact

Email: hello@puzzlegames.space

This address can be used for content corrections, broken link reports, accessibility feedback, or questions about how a specific page is structured.

Rights and source requests

If you are a publisher, rights holder, or representative and you have a question about sourcing, attribution, or removal, contact the site directly by email with the affected URL and the nature of the request.

Formal copyright notices can also follow the process described on the DMCA page.

Game page corrections

If a game page includes an outdated source link, incorrect category placement, or a broken embed path, send the exact page URL and a short description of the issue so it can be reviewed efficiently.

Response scope

Messages related to rights, accessibility, or clear factual errors are prioritized. General recommendations and source suggestions are also welcome when they match the site’s puzzle and browser-game focus.
