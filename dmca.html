DMCA Policy

PuzzleGames.space responds to good-faith copyright concerns and reviews requests related to source attribution, page presentation, or removal.

1. Filing a notice

If you believe that content on PuzzleGames.space infringes your copyright or is being presented in a way that requires review, send a notice to hello@puzzlegames.space.

Include the page URL, the work you claim is affected, your relationship to that work, and enough information to evaluate the request efficiently.

2. Information to provide

A complete notice should include your name, contact information, a clear description of the claimed issue, the exact page or URLs involved, and a statement that the request is made in good faith.

3. Review process

PuzzleGames.space may review the page, adjust attribution or source handling, disable the page, or remove the listing while the request is being evaluated.

4. Repeat concerns

Sources or page types that repeatedly trigger valid rights complaints may be removed from the library entirely.
