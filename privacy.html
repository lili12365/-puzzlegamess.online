Privacy Policy

This policy explains how PuzzleGames.space handles basic site data, embedded content behavior, and information sent through direct contact.

1. Technical information

Like most websites, PuzzleGames.space may record basic technical information through server logs or hosting infrastructure, such as IP address, browser type, device type, referral source, requested pages, and access timestamps.

This information is used for site operation, troubleshooting, security monitoring, and general traffic review.

2. Cookies and local storage

Some parts of the site or embedded third-party content may use cookies, local storage, or similar browser technologies in order to load media, preserve preferences, or support functionality on the source platform.

Browser controls can usually be used to manage or remove cookies according to the settings supported by your device and browser.

3. Embedded content and third-party sources

Some game pages may load embedded content from third-party publishers. When that happens, the third-party provider may receive technical information from your browser and may apply its own cookies or data practices when the embedded content loads or when you interact with it.

Those third-party practices are governed by the privacy terms of the relevant publisher or source host, not by PuzzleGames.space alone.

4. Contact information you send directly

If you email PuzzleGames.space, the information you include in your message may be retained so the request can be reviewed and answered. That can include your email address, the message content, and any URLs or supporting details you provide.

5. Updates

This policy may be updated when site functionality, data handling practices, or third-party integrations change.
